Who is Véloproche ?

Co-founded in 2022, Véloproche is a start-up that allows you to find bikes near you at any time 🚴‍♂️. Several types of bikes are available everywhere in France. From now on, no more traffic jams and pollution. Freedom is yours ! In addition, Véloproche offers other activities such as bike rides with people in the area 🌳. So on weekends you can sign up and join a group for a ride.You can also access secure bicycle routes via our website.
